# Behavior Insights

## Overview

The Insights module surfaces behavioral patterns that explain the user's body composition trajectory. It answers: "Am I actually doing the things that drive results?" — tracking protein adherence, workout consistency, and step counts. It also surfaces the active alerts generated by the decision engine.

## Business Rules

- **Protein adherence %**: `mean(proteinScore) over last 30 days` for days where `proteinIntake` was logged. Days with no intake logged are excluded from the mean (not treated as 0%).
- **Workout consistency %**: `(days worked out in last 30 days / weeklyWorkoutTarget × 30/7) × 100`. Clamped to 100%.
- **Average steps (7d and 30d)**: Mean of `DailyLog.steps` over the respective windows. Null days excluded.
- **Current streak — logged days**: Consecutive days from today backwards where a `DailyLog` entry exists.
- **Current streak — workout days**: Consecutive days from today backwards where `workedOut = true`.
- **Active alerts**: Pulled from `ComputedMetric.alerts` for today's computed row. Multiple alerts can be active simultaneously.
- Alert history: Clicking an alert type shows a timeline of when that alert was first triggered and any recurrences.
